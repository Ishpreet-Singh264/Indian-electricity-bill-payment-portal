About Us

This portal is operated by the Ministry of power, government of india with the co-operation of state electricity boards to provide a hassle-free electricity bill payment experience to the citizens of India. Ministry of power is an Indian government ministry that oversees electricity production and infrastucture projects related to it including generation, transmission, delivery as well as maintenance projects.

Visit powermin.gov.in for more information.
